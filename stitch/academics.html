Departmental Overviews

English Department

Our English department fosters a deep appreciation for literature and develops strong analytical and writing skills. Students engage with a diverse range of texts, from classic literature to contemporary works, and hone their abilities in critical analysis, argumentation, and creative expression.

Mathematics Department

The Mathematics department is dedicated to developing logical reasoning and problem-solving skills. Our curriculum covers a wide spectrum of mathematical concepts, from foundational algebra to advanced calculus, preparing students for success in STEM fields and beyond.

Science Department

Through hands-on experiments and inquiry-based learning, our Science department inspires curiosity and a passion for discovery. Students explore the principles of biology, chemistry, and physics, developing a strong understanding of the natural world and the scientific method.

History Department

Our History department encourages students to think critically about the past and its impact on the present. Through the study of world and American history, students develop research skills, analyze primary sources, and gain a nuanced understanding of historical events and their context.

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is designed to provide a balanced and challenging education, covering core subjects such as English, mathematics, science, social studies, and foreign languages. We also offer a wide range of elective courses to allow students to pursue their interests and passions. Our approach emphasizes inquiry-based learning, collaboration, and real-world application of knowledge.

Educational Approach

At Stitch School, we believe in fostering a student-centered learning environment. Our teachers are dedicated to creating engaging and supportive classrooms where students can thrive. We utilize a variety of teaching methods, including project-based learning, discussions, and hands-on activities, to cater to diverse learning styles and promote active participation. We also integrate technology into our curriculum to enhance learning and prepare students for the digital age.

Academic Support

We offer a variety of academic support services to help students succeed, including tutoring, study groups, and academic advising. Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to providing the resources and guidance students need to reach their full potential.
