Frequently Asked Questions

What are the application deadlines?

expand_more

Our Early Decision deadline is November 1st, and the Regular Decision deadline is January 15th. We also offer rolling admissions for certain programs.

Do you offer financial aid?

expand_more

Yes, we offer a variety of financial aid and scholarship options. We are committed to making an Stitch School education accessible to all qualified students. Please visit our Financial Aid page for more information.

Is an interview required for admission?

expand_more

Interviews are required for some programs and highly recommended for all applicants. It's a great opportunity for us to get to know you better and for you to learn more about our school.

What is the student-to-faculty ratio?

expand_more

Stitch School boasts a low student-to-faculty ratio of 10:1, ensuring personalized attention and a supportive learning environment for all our students.
